Your Name

Kimi no Nawa (Your Name in English) is an anime movie that was released in 2016. This romance animated film became popular at the time of its release. It tells the story of two high school students who turn out to be a couple despite the circumstances. The legend about the red thread says that wherever we are, we will be bound by our soul mate.
